3. Manual Attack Test

Try these attack scenarios to test PAD:

  • Photo Attack: Hold a printed photo of a face in front of the camera
  • Video Replay: Play a video of a face on another device
  • Static Image: Show a static image without movement
  • Mask Attack: Use a realistic mask (if available)

Upload videos of these attacks using the debug tool above.

Expected Results

  • Real Face: High confidence scores (>0.5), no attack detected
  • Photo Attack: Low spatial score, no temporal signal, attack detected
  • Video Replay: Low temporal score (no heart rate), attack detected
  • Static Image: Low pose score, attack detected
